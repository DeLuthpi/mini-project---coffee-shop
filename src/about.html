About us

D'Coffee Shop

Established: 2024


About Us:

D'Coffee Shop is a cozy and inviting coffee haven located in south Bekasi. Since 2024, we have been dedicated to serving our community with exceptional coffee and a warm atmosphere.


Our Mission:

At D'Coffee Shop, our mission is to craft the perfect cup of coffee for every customer, every time. We source the finest beans from around the world and roast them locally to ensure freshness and quality in every sip.


Our Menu:

From classic espresso beverages to specialty lattes and refreshing iced coffees, our menu offers something for everyone. Pair your favorite drink with our selection of freshly baked pastries and light bites for a delightful treat.


Community Engagement:

We believe in giving back to the community that supports us. Through partnerships with local charities and events, we strive to make a positive impact beyond our coffee cups.


Location:

You can find us at Pedurenan village, Puri Gading, Jatiasih, South Bekasi West Java - Indonesia 17425 conveniently located near Puri Gaidng ringroad. Whether you're looking for your morning pick-me-up or a place to unwind with friends, D'Coffee Shop welcomes you to enjoy the perfect blend of coffee and community.


Contact Us:

Visit our website at dcoffeeshop.co.id or follow us on social media [@DCoffeeShop] for the latest updates, promotions, and more.


Join us at D'Coffee Shop, where every cup tells a story of passion, quality, and the love for coffee.
